My Favorite Movie Collection

2021

1. Story of Yanxi Palace (Diên Hi Công Lược)

This drama about a witty and gritty girl who rises from the lowest servant position
to being the most powerful female in the Forbidden City is a delight.

2. Autumn's Concerto (Trạm kế tiếp, hạnh phúc)

The drama explores themes of love, family, and social class, and has become
a cultural phenomenon in Taiwan and beyond.

3. Under The Power (Cẩm Y Chi Hạ)

This drama about heartless Field Commander Lu Yi (Ren Jia Lun) and
sunny constable Yuan Jinxia (Tan Song Yun) is compelling and enjoyable.
